Services

Apple services include entertainment, cloud storage, and technical support.

Entertainment
Apple TV+: Offers original shows and movies
Apple Music: Offers music streaming
Apple Arcade: Offers games
Apple Fitness+: Offers fitness coaching
Apple News+: Offers news and culture updates
Apple Podcasts: Offers podcast
Apple Books: Offers books
Cloud storage
iCloud: Offers paid storage
Technical support
AppleCare+: Offers extended hardware repair coverage, technical support, and accidental damage protection

Apple also offers support for its products, including the iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.
